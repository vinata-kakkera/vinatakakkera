prithiv cafe

i had visited in dec 2018

Prithvi cafe is located beside prithvi theatre near juhu beach. Is an centre of attraction for people visiting juhu and others. Best place to hang out with friends , family and to do informal meetings. I often visit this place as i am attracted to the food served here. As the quality and quantity is the best thing that matters. prithvi cafe serves the best quality food at pocket friendly prices with a good quantity. The ambience here is most pleasant . Open air and with roof decors . Their service is good . The staff is helping
